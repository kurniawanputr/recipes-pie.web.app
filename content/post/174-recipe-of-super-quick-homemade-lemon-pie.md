##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Pie:

1. Make ready  Pie crust
1. Make ready 1 1/4 cup Plain flour
1. Prepare 7 tbsp Butter
1. Make ready 3 tbsp Light sugar
1. Take  Filling
1. Make ready 1/2 can Condensed milk
1. Prepare 1 Lemon
1. Prepare  Topping
1. Take 50 grams Whipping cream powder
1. Prepare 100 ml Milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Lemon Pie:

1. Put the butter and the sugar into a batter.
1. Mix it until combined. In the low speed, add the flour.
1. Mix it really well and combine the dough with your hands, making a ball.
1. Put it into a 25 cm pan, and put it in the oven at high temperature for 15-18 minutes.
1. Make the filling: mix the condensed milk with the lemon juice, making a thick cream.
1. Now, put the Whipping powder into a batter with the milk and mix it for about 6-8 minutes.
1. Put the filling in the pie crust, top with the whipping cream and put it in the fridge until you want to serve it.
