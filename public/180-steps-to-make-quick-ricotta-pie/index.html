The ingredients needed to make Ricotta pie:
  1. Make ready 2-15 oz ricotta
  2. Make ready 1 an 1/2 cups milk
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Take 1 vanilla bean
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Mexican vanilla
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  7. Take 1/2 cup sugar in the raw
  8. Take 1/8 tsp lemon oil, or 1 teaspoon of lemon extract
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp cornstarch
  10. Take Lemon zest
  11. Get Cinnamon powder
  12. Make ready Fresh nutmeg
  13. Make ready 2 regular pie shells
