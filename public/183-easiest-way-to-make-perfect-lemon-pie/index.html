The ingredients needed to make Lemon pie:
  1. Make ready Filling
  2. Get 3 egg yolks
  3. Get 1 can (14oz) Eagle Brand sweet ended condensed milk
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup lemon juice
  5. Prepare 1 baked pie crust
  6. Get meringue
  7. Take 6 egg whites
  8. Get 2 tsp vanilla extract or cream of tartar
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar

Steps to make Lemon pie:
  1. Preheat oven 325°F. Beat egg yolks, gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk & lemon juice.
  2. Pour into crust. Bake 30-35 minutes or until set.
  3. To make Meringue: Beat egg whites on high really good. Add vanilla & sugar. Continue to beat on high till a non-glossy look is achieved.
  4. After pie is cooked, add meringue to top and cook in oven till tips are brown.
