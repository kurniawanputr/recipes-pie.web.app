The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie EASY:
  1. Take Crust
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups fine crushed graham crackers
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 6 TBS melted butter
  5. Prepare Filling
  6. Take 28 oz sweet condensed milk (2 cans)
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream
  8. Get 3/4 cup key lime juice
  9. Take Zest of 2 Limes or 4 Key Limes
  10. Make ready Whipped cream topping
  11. Make ready 1 cup heavy cream
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  13. Take 1 tsp vanilla
