The ingredients needed to make Butterfinger Pie:
  1. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  2. Get 1/2 cup peanut butter
  3. Get 2 tsp vanilla
  4. Take 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  5. Get 1 (12.5 oz) bag fun sized butterfinger crushed (save 2 for on top)
  6. Prepare 8 oz cool whip
  7. Make ready 1 chocolate ready made pie crust
Instructions to make Butterfinger Pie:
  1. Beat cream cheese and peanut butter until smooth
  2. Add in vanilla and powdered sugar
  3. Fold in candy bars and cool whip
  4. Spread in pie crust and top with last of candy
  5. Refrigerate for 4 hours
