The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie:
  1. Get Crust
  2. Make ready 2 cups (10 oz) flour
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1/3 cup butter
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup shortening
  7. Take 1/2 cup water
  8. Make ready Filling
  9. Get 7-9 pie apples
  10. Make ready 3/4-1 cup sugar
  11. Get 2 Tbsp flour
  12. Make ready 2 tsp cinnamon
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  14. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  15. Make ready 1/8 tsp ground allspice
  16. Make ready 3 Tbsp butter
  17. Prepare 2-3 Tbsp milk
  18. Get 1/4 cup cinnamon sugar (1 tsp cinnamon + 1/4 cup sugar)
Instructions to make Apple Pie:
  1. Sift together flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Cut butter and shortening into 1/2 in pieces. Add to flour mixture. Cut or rub into flour until the largest pieces are no bigger than a pea.
  3. Add water by the tablespoon, stirring gently until dough comes together.
  4. Form pie dough into a ball and wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 min while making the filling.
  5. Peel, core, and cut apples into 1/4 in slices. Place in a large bowl.
  6. In a small bowl, combine sugar, 2 Tbsp flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. Sprinkle over apple slices and gently toss to combine. Let sit and get juicy while rolling out the crust.
  7. Roll out half the pie dough to 2"wider than the pie plate (3" for deep dish). Gently drape into plate, making sure there are no air bubbles. Pour apples on top and arrange evenly. Cover with accumulated juices.
  8. Slice remaining 3 Tbsp butter into thin pats and arrange over filling.
  9. Roll out remaining half of pie dough to roughly the same size as the previous half. Cut vents in desired pattern. Gently drape over pie and crimp edges.
  10. Brush entire pie with milk and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  11. Bake at 400° for 50-60 min, tenting with foil if crust is browning too quickly. Filling should be bubbling when you remove it.
  12. Let rest at least 30 min to 1 hour before cutting into it. Best enjoyed with whipped cream or ice cream.
