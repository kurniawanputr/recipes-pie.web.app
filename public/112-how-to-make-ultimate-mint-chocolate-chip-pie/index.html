  1. Make ready 1 oreo or chocolate pie crust
  2. Make ready 1 1/4 cup heavy cream
  3. Prepare 8 oz. Softened cream cheese
  4. Prepare 1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  5. Prepare Few drops green food coloring
  6. Take 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 3/4 tsp peppermint extract
  8. Take 1 cup mini chocolate chips
  9. Take 1 cup Andes candy

Instructions to make Mint Chocolate Chip Pie:
  1. Beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form
  2. Beat cream cheese for 2 minutes, add food coloring and powdered sugar beat until smooth. Then add vanilla extract and peppermint extract until combined.
  3. Fold in heavy cream then mini chocolate chips and finally crushed Andes candy.
  4. Put mixture in pie crust and top with chocolate chips and Andes candy and drizzle with melted chocolate.
  5. Refrigerate for 5 - 6 hours until firm
