Instructions to make Mango pie:
  1. Bowl ma maidah, salt, sugar, butter or egg dal kar mix kary
  2. Milk ki madad se soft dough ready kar lay.
  3. Dough ko half hour kay liye rest dey room temperature par.
  4. Pan ma mango or sugar dal kar paka lay, jab mango achi tarah se mash hu jaya tu flame off kar daay.
  5. Ready dough ki 2 rotiya bana lay
  6. Grees kiya huwa pan ma roti rakh kar andar mango ki filling kary.
  7. Ab dosri roti par neechan laga lar filling kay upar rakh kar side's se baand kar dey or egg brush kar dey
  8. Phaly se pre heat oven ma 150 C par bake kar lay.
