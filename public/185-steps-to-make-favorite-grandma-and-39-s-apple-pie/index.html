The ingredients needed to make Grandma's Apple Pie:
  1. Make ready for filling
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Take 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  6. Get 1 pinch salt
  7. Make ready 6 cup apples, peeled, cored and sliced, and tossed in lemon juice to preserve color
  8. Take 1 milk for brushing on before baking
  9. Make ready 1 lemon juice
  10. Take pie crust
  11. Take 2 cup flour
  12. Get 2 stick butter, room temperature
  13. Get 1/3 cup water
Instructions to make Grandma's Apple Pie:
  1. Make pie crust: Cut butter into the flour in tiny pieces. I mix it with the flour until the whole thing looks like crumbs. I am not afraid to use my (clean) hands, though gloves can be worn.
  2. Add the water, a little at a time, while kneading briefly until play dough consistency is achieved. Shape into a ball,cover the bowl and set aside.
  3. Mix the following:
  4. Stir in the prepared apples
  5. Roll out the crust and place it into a 9 inch pan. Reserve some dough for the lattice work later on.
  6. Add apple mix.
  7. Roll out left-over dough, cut into 1/2 inch strips, and artfully criss-cross them over the filling. Lightly moisten the parts that will need to stick to each other.
  8. Lightly brush with milk.
  9. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes.
  10. Turn down oven to 350°F for 45 minutes.
  11. Enjoy while it is still warm!
