The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Butterscotch Pie:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter, melted
  2. Get 1 cup sugar substitute, such as splenda/truvia (can also use reg sugar)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Rice flour (or whatever flour you would like)
  4. Make ready 3 tsp egg replacement powder
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp warm water
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  8. Take 1/2 cup butterscotch chips
  9. Prepare 1 unbaked pie crust
Instructions to make Chocolate Butterscotch Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. In mixing bowl, beat butter, sugar substitute, flour, & vanilla until well blended.
  2. In small bowl, combine egg replacement & water. Mix until combined well. Then add to other mixture & mix well.
  3. Add chocolate chips & butterscotch chips. Mix well. Pour into pie crust.
  4. Bake for 1 hr or until golden brown. Let cool.
