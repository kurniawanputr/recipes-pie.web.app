The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Slab something or other:
  1. Prepare Filling
  2. Get 10 cups granny smith apples, peeled & sliced
  3. Make ready 1 cup brown sugar; if you like it sweeter, add 1/2 cup more
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  5. Make ready Pie Crust
  6. Get 2 cups all purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. Get 12 tablespoons cubed butter
  10. Get 6-8 tablespoons ice water
  11. Take Crumble Topping
  12. Take 4 tablespoons cubed butter
  13. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  14. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  15. Get Greased 9x13in. baking pan
Steps to make Apple Pie Slab something or other:
  1. Crust: In a large bowl whisk together flour, sugar, & salt
  2. Work 4 tablespoons of butter into the dry mixture, with a fork til it resembles corn meal
  3. Add the rest of the butter in & mix
  4. Pour in 6 tablespoons of water to start & try to knead the dough together
  5. Dump out mixture onto a floured surface & continue kneading, just until combined
  6. Form dough into a disc, cover in plastic wrap & refrigerate for a minimum of 30 minutes
  7. Now's a good time to preheat your oven to 425Â°
  8. Apple Filling: Wash, peel & thinly slice apples
  9. In another large bowl, toss in apples, sugar & cinnamon to mix together, then set aside
  10. Crumb Topping: in a medium bowl whisk together the flour & brown sugar
  11. Work 4 tablespoons butter into flour mixture with a fork until it resembles corn meal
  12. Assembly: Roll out pie dough into a 10x14 rectangle
  13. Transfer into your buttered cake pan making sure there's no air pockets underneath or holes in the dough; I used a nonstick 9x13in. pan
  14. Place your apple mixture into the crust & distribute them evenly
  15. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the apples evenly
  16. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until juices have slightly thickened & are bubbly
  17. Let set for 2 hours at room temperature or overnight in the fridge
