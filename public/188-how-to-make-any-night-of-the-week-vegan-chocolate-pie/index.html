The ingredients needed to make Vegan chocolate pie:
  1. Get For the crust:
  2. Make ready 4 units dried apricots
  3. Make ready 3 units dates (no seeds)
  4. Prepare 200 g Brazilian nuts or almonds
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons shredded coconut
  7. Take For the filling:
  8. Make ready 200 g dark chocolate (vegan)
  9. Make ready 150 g coconut milk
  10. Take 20 g sugar (opcional)
  11. Make ready For the jam:
  12. Get 250 g strawberries (frozen or not)
  13. Prepare 200 g raspberries (frozen or not)
  14. Get Sugar as much you like

Steps to make Vegan chocolate pie:
  1. In a food processor, put all the ingredients for the crust and process until it looks like crumbs.
  2. Put the crust in a pan with removable bottom and press with your fingers all over the bottom and sides. You can put in small pans too, it looks cute
  3. Take it to the oven (180° C, pre heated) until it looks golden. Let it cool.
  4. Heat the chocolate and coconut milk until it gets creamy. Mix in sugar. Let it cool and pour over the pie. Take it to the fridge to get firm.
  5. Make the jam: mix the berries and sugar and cook until they get almost melted. Smash them to jam texture. Let it cool. Pour over the pie and take it to the fridge. Serve cool.
