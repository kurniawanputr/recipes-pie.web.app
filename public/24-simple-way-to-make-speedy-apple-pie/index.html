  5. Take 60 gm Butter chilled
  6. Get 2 tbsp Milk chilled

  8. Take 1 Apple big(chopped thin)

  10. Make ready 1 pinch Nutmeg powder

  12. Get 1/2 tsp Cinnamon powder
