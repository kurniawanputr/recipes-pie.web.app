The ingredients needed to make Simple pasta pie:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup pasta (any)
  2. Take 500 grams Mozarella cheese
  3. Take 200 grams Cheddar cheese
  4. Get 3 large potatoes
  5. Prepare 300 grams minced meat (any)
  6. Take 5 tbsp Pizza sauce
  7. Get 6 tbsp pasta sauce
  8. Get 2 tsp soya sauce
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Rock salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper crushed
  11. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp oregano
  13. Make ready 1 pie crust

Instructions to make Simple pasta pie:
  1. Potato filling: Cook potatoes half way n then shred. In a mixing bowl mix shredded potatoes with cheddar cheese , rock salt, black pepper. Set aside.
  2. Meat filling: Mix minced meat with soya sauce n pizza sauce. Cook until dry n done. Set aside.
  3. Pasta filling: Cook pasta with generous amount of salt. Drain. In a mixing bowl mix pasta with 250 gms of Mozarella cheese, garlic powder, pasta sauce. Set aside.
  4. Preheat oven.
  5. In a generously greased deep baking pan lay out the pie crust. Drop in potato mixture to form first layer. Then drop in meat mixture to form 2nd layer. Then drop in pasta mixture to make th final layer. Top with 250 gms of Mozarella cheese n sprinkle with oregano.
  6. Bake at 180Â°c for about half n hour. Serve hot.
