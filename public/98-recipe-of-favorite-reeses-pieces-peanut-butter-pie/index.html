The ingredients needed to make Reeses pieces peanut butter pie:
  1. Make ready 1 cup powdered (confectioners) sugar
  2. Take 1 cup peanut butter
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  4. Get 8 oz cream cheese (1/2 bar)
  5. Prepare 8 oz cool whip
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  7. Take 1/2 cup crushed reeses pieces
  8. Get 1 chocolate pie crust

Instructions to make Reeses pieces peanut butter pie:
  1. Put cream cheese on a bowl and cream it
  2. Add powered sugar little by little until well blended
  3. Add your peanut butter, vanilla extract,dmilk and an 1/2 container of cool whip and mix
  4. Incorporate your crushed reeses pieces and pour into the pie crust
  5. Decorate with the leftover cool whip, whole reeses pieces and even some chocolate syrup.
  6. Chill on freezer for at least 2 hrs before serving
