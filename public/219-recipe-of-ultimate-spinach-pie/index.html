  6. Make ready 2/3 stick butter
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp pepper
  9. Take 1 creole season
  10. Take 1 chopped onion
  11. Get 1/2 clove garlic
  12. Take 1 pie crust

Instructions to make spinach pie:
  1. preheat oven to 250
  2. dry out spinach on paper towels
  3. cook ground beef and season with onion, garlic, Creole season and pepper
  4. strain ground beef
  5. cook spinach and season with garlic and Creole season and pepper and salt
  6. peak and cook potatoes
  7. remove water from pot and mash potatoes, then add milk and butter.
  8. let potatoes cool before adding to pie crust
  9. add layer of cheese
  10. add ground beef
  11. add spinach than layer of ground beef again
  12. top off with layer of cheese
  13. slow cook in oven for 30 mins
